About Us


Societech is a website that mainly talks about Press Freedom; a social issue that affects mostly journalists, broadcasters, and other people in the field of Journalism. Press Freedom holds the people to publish information and their expressions without the fear, and the hindrance of facing potential threats, harassments, and red-tagging.


Societech's developers are composed of the collaboration of Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS) and Information and Communication Technology (ICT) students, who are committed to raise awareness and fight for the truth.


In this context we aim to trundle deep information and impart advantages about having a safe environment for the journalists and aspiring future journalists.
